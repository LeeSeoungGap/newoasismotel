CHILDERS OASIS MOTEL 

UNIQUE & CENTRAL BOUTIQUE ACCOMMODATION IN CHILDERS QUEENSLAND

Oasis Motel is your modern, quiet & relaxing accommodation in the center of Childers. In fact, we are the only accommodation conveniently located right in the center of Childers without the highway road noise. Half-way between Brisbane & Rockhampton, Oasis Motel is a great place for an overnight stop on your travel to and from, or simply a weekend away.

For your comfort, our facilities provide Free Austar, Free High Speed WIFI, Heating & Cooling Air Conditioning & Carpark with 24 hour surveillance. You are also welcome to sit back and relax in our beautiful courtyard and lounge area, or entertain yourself by taking a swim in our cool and refreshing inground pool. For your convenience, we also have a fully serviced shared kitchen, BBQ area and laundromat onsite for our guests to use.

Companies are most welcome to leave your contractors or representatives with us for a single night or the whole year. Along with corporate rates on offer, we even have a gym located next door to our motel.

Oasis Motel is a family owned and operated business that makes you feel at home, and is truly your unique & boutique hideaway in Childers. A great venue for any function and the most perfect place for you to enjoy the real ‘oasis’.

Welcome and we look forward to greeting you in our beautiful town of Childers.
